Reverse-engineered from the designers' own statics

From plain to scroll-stopping

The first pass was clean but read as a plain social post. Studying the real Natural Force designer ads showed why theirs stop the scroll, and it is a repeatable formula. These two clone their proven layouts with collagen swapped in.

The 100% formula

  1. Hero product, big and floating on a clean white or cream ground, not tucked into a lifestyle scene.
  2. A signature element from the product's own world. Their Pure C8 gets a flame plume, Organic MCT gets coconuts. Collagen gets a coffee-and-cream swirl.
  3. One bold graphic device carries the concept: the toggle, the quiz pills, the pine-green arch.
  4. Big confident serif headline, minimal copy, generous negative space.
  5. Brand as a light skin: cream and white with deep pine-green, no orange (that is Pure C8 only).
