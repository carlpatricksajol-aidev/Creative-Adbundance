Before you write —

Maybe we've already answered.

The questions people ask most often. If yours isn't here, the form's right above this.

Can I edit my gift after it's made?

Yes — reorder photos, swap clips, regenerate the song, or change the title from your dashboard. No need to write us.

Where is my gift link?

Check the email we sent when it finished generating — subject line "Your gift is ready." If you can't find it, write us with the recipient's name and we'll dig it up.

I want to delete a gift or my account.

Just send the word "delete" plus your email from this form, and we'll wipe everything within 24 hours. No questions asked.

Are the songs really custom?

Yes. Four original songs per gift, with lyrics written from your answers — their name, your memory, your inside jokes. No stock library, no templates.

How long does it actually take?

Most gifts are ready in 8–12 minutes. We email you the moment yours is done, and the link works forever — so you can review it before sending, no rush.

What if I don't love what you make?

You see everything before you pay. The free tier lets you preview a watermarked version — only upgrade if it's a "yes."

Does the recipient need an account?

No. They tap the link and the gift plays. That's it. No signup, no app, no friction.
