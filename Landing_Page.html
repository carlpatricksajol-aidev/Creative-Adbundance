The questions
everyone asks first.

Yes, you can edit it. Yes, your photos are private. Yes, the song is actually custom.

How long does it actually take?

Most gifts are ready in 8–12 minutes. We email you the moment yours is finished, and the link works forever — so you can review it before sending, no rush.

Are the songs really custom?

Yes. We generate four original songs per gift, with lyrics written from your answers — their name, your memory, your inside jokes. No stock library, no templates.

What about my photos? Are they private?

Your uploads are encrypted, never used to train anything, and you can delete them at any time. Recipient pages are unlisted by default — only people with the exact link can view. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I edit it after it's made?

Yes. Reorder photos, swap clips, regenerate the song, or change the title — all from your dashboard. Premium plans get unlimited regenerations.

What if I don't love what you make?

You see everything before you pay. The free tier lets you generate a watermarked preview — only upgrade if it's a "yes."

Does the recipient need an account?

No. They tap the link and the gift plays. That's it. No signup, no app, no friction.
