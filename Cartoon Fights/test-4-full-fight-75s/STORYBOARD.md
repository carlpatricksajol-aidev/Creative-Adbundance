# Storyboard: "Sponge Fighter vs The Green Gladiator" (full fight, ~75s)

Concept: 3D cinematic (test-3 look), full fight arc with a winner. 5 scenes x 15s Seedance 2.0 clips, stitched. Every scene ENDS holding an exact pose and the next scene OPENS on that same pose, so the cuts land as invisible match cuts.

## Characters (variant designs, filter-safe and legally distinct)

- SPONGE FIGHTER: bright-yellow rectangular sponge, porous texture, buck teeth, aviator goggles (up on forehead until Scene 2, down after), small flowing RED CAPE, white karate gloves, blue shorts, black shoes. Powers: lightning karate, giant soap bubbles, squash-flat-and-spring-back resilience.
- THE GREEN GLADIATOR: colossal green-skinned muscle giant, bronze spiked pauldron on left shoulder, leather arm bracers, white tribal war paint on chest and face, ragged dark-gray shorts, barefoot. Tools and powers: a massive two-handed STONE WAR HAMMER, earth-shattering strength, two-handed thunderclap shockwave, skyscraper leap into ground-pound, rage mode (war paint glows green, muscles swell).

Setting all scenes: ancient stone ruins arena, mountain plateau, storm dusk, heavy rain, puddles, mist, broken pillars, distant fire glow. Teal-orange cinematic grade, volumetric rain, film grain, no HUD, no text. Clean PG action: no blood, impacts read as water spray, mist, shockwaves, sparks.

## Why this stitches perfectly

1. Match-cut poses: scene N's final held composition is written verbatim as scene N+1's opening composition.
2. Identical character, setting, and grade blocks pasted into every prompt (proven consistency technique from tests 1-3).
3. Seedance 2.0 chosen for its shot-list adherence (it follows timestamped beats most faithfully of the models tested).
4. Post: 0.4s fade-in, hard cuts on the matched poses, 0.9s fade-out, 2.39:1 letterbox, single re-encode.

## Fallback rules

- If a scene fails moderation: one reword retry for that scene only, pushing the design further from the source characters.
- Higgsfield (preferred for on-model characters via its ip_detected reveal flow) is not connected in this session; this run uses MaxFusion Seedance 2.0 with the variant designs instead. No KIE.
