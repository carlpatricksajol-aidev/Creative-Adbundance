Questions everyone asks first 🤔

Most gifts are ready in 8–12 minutes. We email you the moment yours is done, and the link works forever.

Yes! Four original songs per gift, lyrics written from your answers. No stock library.

Are my photos private?+

Encrypted, never used to train anything, deletable anytime. Recipient pages are unlisted by default.

Can I edit it after?+

Yep — reorder photos, swap clips, regenerate the song, or change the title from your dashboard.

What if I don't love it?+

You see everything before you pay. Free watermarked preview — upgrade only if it's a "yes."

Does the recipient need an account?+

Nope. They tap the link and the gift plays. No signup, no app.
